The Squat


What qualifies as a successful squat, or legal squat, in powerlifting standards? The IPF rule book states:

“…the lifter must bend the knees and lower the body until the top surface of the legs at the hip joint is lower than the top of the knees.”

What qualifies as a successful bench press in most top powerlifting federations? The USPA rule book states:

“…the lifter must lower the bar to the chest and hold it motionless on the chest with a definite and visible pause…”

The Deadlift

The deadlift might be the most simple lift of the big three. You must lift the weight from a dead stop until you are fully standing tall, shoulders back, and knees completely locked out. There can be no downward bar movement and absolutely zero hitching is allowed.
