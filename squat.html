The Squat

Whether you are squatting to increase strength for olympic style lifting or powerlifting, finding the right technique is crucial in maximizing your potential. Everything from bar placement (low bar vs. high bar), to stance (space between feet and the angle that the big toe points), to depth (ass to grass or just breaking the legal powerlifting plane) depends on the individual.
